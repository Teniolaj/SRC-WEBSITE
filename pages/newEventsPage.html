At SRC Sports Academy, we are excited to announce the addition of four dynamic sports to our ever-growing list of offerings: wrestling, taekwondo, judo, and volleyball. These new activities are more than just sports; they are pathways to personal growth, discipline, and community engagement.

Wrestling

Wrestling is one of the oldest and most demanding combat sports, combining physical strength, agility, and strategy. At SRC Sports Academy, we are introducing wrestling to offer our athletes a unique opportunity to develop resilience, discipline, and tactical thinking. This sport not only challenges the body but also sharpens the mind, teaching participants how to overcome adversity through determination and skill.

  • Monday & Wednesday: 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM
  • Friday: 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Taekwondo

Taekwondo, a Korean martial art, is renowned for its emphasis on high, fast kicks and powerful strikes. It instills discipline, respect, and self-confidence in its practitioners. By introducing taekwondo to SRC Sports Academy, we aim to provide students with a strong foundation in self-defense and the principles of martial arts. This sport also encourages the development of physical fitness, flexibility, and mental fortitude.

  • Tuesday & Thursday: 5:00 PM - 7:00 PM
  • Saturday: 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM

Judo

Judo, which means "the gentle way," is a martial art that emphasizes throwing techniques, joint locks, and grappling. At SRC Sports Academy, judo is introduced to promote the values of humility, respect, and perseverance. This sport not only builds physical strength and coordination but also teaches important life skills such as patience, balance, and self-control.

  • Monday & Thursday: 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM
  • Saturday: 1:00 PM - 3:00 PM

Volleyball

Volleyball is a fast-paced team sport that requires coordination, communication, and strategic thinking. By introducing volleyball at SRC Sports Academy, we are creating an opportunity for athletes to develop teamwork and leadership skills while enjoying a sport that is both competitive and fun. Volleyball helps in building cardiovascular endurance, agility, and sharp reflexes, making it a great addition to our sports offerings.

  • Wednesday & Friday: 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM
  • Sunday: 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM

About SRC Sports Academy

The SRC Sports Academy is dedicated to promoting sports excellence and fostering a sense of community through various sporting activities. Funded by community and national sports bodies, we offer a range of sports programs to enhance physical fitness and teamwork.
