About the SRC Sports Academy

About us

The SRC Sports Academy is dedicated to nurturing talent and fostering a spirit of excellence, teamwork, and sportsmanship within our university and the local community. Through a diverse range of sports programs, including football, rugby, ballet, table tennis, handball, gymnastics, and newly introduced activities like wrestling, taekwondo, judo, and volleyball, we aim to provide a supportive and inclusive environment where athletes can thrive and achieve their full potential.

Quote from the SRC President

“Our vision is to create a premier sports institution that not only uplifts the image of our university but also strengthens the bonds within our community. We believe in the power of sports to inspire, empower, and transform lives. Together, we can achieve greatness on and off the field.” — [Mr Paul Desmond]

SRC Sports Academy Mission and Vision

Mission Statement

At the SRC Sports Academy, our mission is to empower students and the community through comprehensive sports programs that promote physical fitness, personal growth, and community engagement. We are committed to fostering a culture of excellence, teamwork, and sportsmanship by providing high-quality training, resources, and support for athletes at all levels.

Vision Statement

Our vision is to become a premier sports institution in the region, recognized for our dedication to developing talented athletes and nurturing future leaders. We aspire to create an inclusive environment where everyone, regardless of their background, can pursue their passion for sports. By continuously expanding our programs and strengthening our community ties, we aim to inspire a lifelong love of sports and make a positive impact on the lives of our students and the local community.
